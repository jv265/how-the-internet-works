Domain Names and ICANN

Definitions

Domain Names:
A domain name can be thought of a way of identifying an IP address where internet users can access one's site. They help create an impression on your site for visitors and its the first interaction they make with your website. Also known as a brand identifier, it tells potential users or customers what the focus of your site is and its relevance.
